Engineering-Grade Tools

Built for roboticists, by roboticists. No installation required.

Gemini AI Copilot

Describe your motion task in plain English ("Pick up the red box") and let the AI generate optimized kinematic scripts automatically.

URDF Import

Drag and drop standard URDF files to visualize your custom hardware. Full support for meshes, joint limits, and kinematic chains.

Driver Export

Prototype in the browser, deploy to the edge. Export verified motion paths directly to C++ (Arduino/ESP32) or Python.

ROS 2 Bridge

Seamlessly integrate with the Robot Operating System. Broadcast TF trees and subscribe to joint states over WebSockets.

Real-Time IK

Advanced Jacobian-based Inverse Kinematics solver allows for interactive, drag-and-drop end-effector control.

Web Serial

Connect directly to your microcontroller via USB from Chrome. Send joint angles to physical hardware in real-time.
